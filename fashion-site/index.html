ACT I AZURE
The Morning Square

AZURE

A coat in the color of open sky.

Story

The first hour after sunset, when light softens and the world breathes out. Tailoring built on the geometry of twilight: shoulders lowered, lapels narrowed, the suit worn as a second skin.

Fabric
  • Virgin wool, brushed handle, 420 g/m².
  • Mulberry silk lining, midnight-dyed.
  • Cashmere, woven in a micro-herringbone.
Silhouette

Long jacket, dropped shoulder, a single closed button. Trousers pleated once, falling to the ankle.

Looks — 12
Runway — Paris, 03.III.MMXXV Director — N. Almara Score — Jóhann J.

The night does not end.
It simply rewrites itself.
